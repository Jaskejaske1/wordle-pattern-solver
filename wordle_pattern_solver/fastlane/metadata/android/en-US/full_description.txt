A powerful, cross-platform utility to help you solve Wordle reasoning puzzles.

Features:
* **Solver Logic**: Enter your guesses and colors to filter the remaining possible words.
* **Daily Solution**: Fetches today's NYT solution (with spoiler protection).
* **Pattern Saving**: Save difficult game states to analyze later.
* **Smart Filtering**: Supports Green (Correct), Yellow (Wrong Position), and Grey (Absent) constraints.
* **Responsive Design**: Works great on both Phones and Tablets.

Completely free and open source. No ads, no tracking.
